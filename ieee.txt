CHAPTER 1: THE PULSE OF THE EARTH (OUR HARDWARE)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.1 Sensors: The Stethoscope for Your Soil
In traditional farming, you have to guess the soil's health by looking at it. Krishi Kaar changes 
that. Our sensors act like a doctor's stethoscope. They are placed deep in the earth to listen to 
the heartbeat of your field. 
- Moisture Sensors: They tell us if the soil is comfortably damp or dangerously dry.
- Temperature & Humidity: They track the very air your plants breathe.

1.2 The Arduino: The Language Translator
Computers speak "Binary" (zeros and ones). Plants speak through "Signals." Our Arduino hardware 
acts as the Master Translator. It takes the silent signals from the dirt and translates them into 
human language that our AI can understand.

1.3 Plug-and-Play (Digital Magic)
We know you have a farm to run and don't have time to be a computer engineer. That’s why we built 
"Auto-Discovery." The moment you plug your sensors into your computer, Krishi Kaar automatically 
finds them, greets them, and starts working. No setup, no confusion, just magic.

CHAPTER 2: THE MASTER FARMER (OUR AI BRAIN)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Behind the screen of Krishi Kaar lives an incredibly smart AI. Think of it as a "Master Farmer" 
who has read every book on agriculture and studied millions of farms.

2.1 The Perfect Marriage (Crop Recommendation)
Every soil has a soulmate crop. Some soil loves Rice; others dream of Coffee. Our AI looks at 
the nutrients (NPK), the weather, and the moisture to find that "Perfect Marriage." We don't just 
give you a list; we identify the "Highly Recommended" crop—the one that has the best chance 
of making you the most profit with the least risk.

2.2 Precision Medicine (Expert Fertilizer Advisory)
For years, farmers have used the same fertilizer (like Urea) for everything. This is like a 
doctor giving the same pill for every headache. Krishi Kaar is smarter. 
Our "Expert Logic Layer" watches your soil. If you already have enough Nitrogen, the AI will 
bravely step in and say, "Wait! No more Urea. Use DAP instead." This is precision medicine for 
the earth, ensuring your soil stays healthy for generations.

2.3 The Daily Water Target (Volumetric Irrigation)
Instead of just saying "The pump is ON," Krishi Kaar calculates a "Daily Target." It tells you 
exactly how many liters of water your field needs today based on the heat and the soil type. This 
saves your water and protects your crops from drowning.
